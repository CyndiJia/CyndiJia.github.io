Cyndi Jia is an interaction designer and creative technologist, with interests in exploring communications between human and emerging media techniques. She also holds an interest in human-centered design. Her works focus on but not limited to: Interactive Installation, Creative coding, Web Development and Storytelling.

Currently major in Interactive Media Arts at NYU Shanghai and minor in Sound and Music Computing at NYU Abu Dhabi.
